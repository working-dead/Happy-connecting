Home About Us

Back in 2 years, we never thought that we'll be forced to stay in our homes, all day everyday. Cook by ourselves, kill time by ourselves, work by ourselves, and not have the basic human interaction. This pandemic has made us realise the value of socialisation, the value of seeking friends, the value of keeping up with our sanity but the problem arises that most of us don't know how to come out of our comfort zones and interact, or sometimes we don't want to bond with people who are already there, people whom we know! That's where our role comes into play, We're here to make it all easy for you, to eradicate all your blues, and fear of socialising And we want you to know, that you're not alone in this, we are all in this together! SO, come, let's watch, play, work, and cook together, let's be each other's home away from home.
